Snape
Severus Snape, a character in J.K Rowling's Harry Potter series, is my favorite virtual character of all time. What attracts me most is perhaps the mysterious complexity lying inside him: frailty and strength, ordinary and greatness, self-abasement and self-conceit, the yearning of power and the empathy towards the weak, each of these contradictory attribute co-exist as part of him. To some extent, I seek in him the richness of humanity, and each time I reread the story of him, I come up with some new understandings of human-beings. For me, he's already like an old friend of mine, and his story never ends with the book.
