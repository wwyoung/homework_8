Stockholm
Among all the cities I've lived in, Stockholm is my favorite. Partially because it's the very first foreign city I've visited in my life, from where I started to measure the world. I still remember the moment when that 20-year-old me landed on Stockholm in the summer twilight, and was enchanted by its sweetness at the first sight. For me, Stockholm is a city of natural beauty and social inclusiveness as well. Yet among all its characters, what fascinates me most is its extreme seasonal shift of day and night. I feel that Stockholm is like a sensitive creature with strong emotions and various facades, and each and every facade has its unique charm.
